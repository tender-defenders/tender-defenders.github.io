We collect minimal personal information necessary to provide you with our gaming services:

2. HOW DO WE USE YOUR INFORMATION?

We use your information to:

3. HOW DO WE PROTECT YOUR INFORMATION?

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no online method of transmission is 100% secure. We recommend accessing our services only through secure networks.

4. HOW LONG DO WE KEEP YOUR INFORMATION?

We retain your information only for as long as you maintain an active account with us or as required by law. When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your information unless required to retain it for legal purposes.

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:
